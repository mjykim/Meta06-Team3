THE FINAL PRODUCT

After many iterations, I created a friction fit clamp that
slides onto the edges of Poppin’s Series A desk system.

Powder Coated Steel Bar Stock

The Real Thing

This was the final visual and functional prototype
that I created. While the real manufactured product
would likely be injection molded, I worked with a
welder to fabricate this prototype from steel in
three of Poppin’s colors.

BRANCH IN USE

"The Money Shot"

With three final prototypes, I was able to provide
Poppin with a way to visualize an office environment
where each employee had a desk clamp. Everyone
loved how Branch uniquely identified each
employee’s space while providing visual interest and
pops of color to the office as a whole.
