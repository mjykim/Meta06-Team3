Where it all started...

PROJECT GOAL

Create an innovative storage solution for
personal items in a collaborative office setting

RESEARCH

I surveyed employees office behavior to gain a better
understanding of what exactly people bring to the
office everyday. While I did find some unexpected items
like bicycles and dogs, the majority of employees
reported bringing bags and jackets to work daily.

With these findings, I decided to explore storage
solutions specifically for organizing bags and jackets.

PROJECT GOAL

Create an innovative storage solution for
personal items in a collaborative office setting

IDEATION

DESK CLAMP

Utilizes the edges of desks by
providing employees with a 15”-18”
slim friction clamp to hang bags and
jackets right at their side.

Pros?

Keeps the office environment clean
while defining each employee
space with a unique slim column

SOFA STORAGE

Adds a standing-height counter to
Poppin’s stadium-like Block Party
Sofa and provides storage underneath

Pros?

Elevates the collaboration in the
office while also providing a simple
storage solution

DESK CUBBY

Adds a personal storage cubby to
the end cap of Poppin's current
desk system

Pros?

Mamizes office space by utilizing
the dead space in the existing
desk format

BRANCH

MY DESIGN DIRECTION

Ultimately, I decided that the desk clamp was my strongest solution because
it provided employees with convenient storage with without blocking the sight
lines of the room. I reviewed each of the sketches with Poppin and they loved
the simple hook solution too!

COMPETITIVE RESEARCH

#workupwards

Currently, Poppin only provides
employees with storage on top or
underneath their workspace. I
really wanted to do something
different by exploring vertical
storage. I began researching simple
products and mechanisms that
creatively utilized the limited real
estate of office environments.
